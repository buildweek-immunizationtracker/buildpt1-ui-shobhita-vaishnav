Don’t Get Caught! Get The Shot!

Immunization Tracker is designed to help individuals and families assess which vaccines they need based on their age and health conditions and keep track of immunizations as they receive them. After all, good record-keeping begins with good record-taking.

As a parent, it's hard to keep track of your child's immunization records! Especially if you move states, or switch doctors. It's a pain to call around and figure out which immunizations your child still needs, or to have them mail you proof for things like school registration. Keeping up-to-date immunization records for your family, especially your children, is important. You will need your children’s immunization records to register them for school, child care, athletic teams, and summer camps or to travel.

As an adult, it's even harder to access these records, as the years pass by.

Immunization Tracker allows medical professionals to upload immunization records to your personal or family account.
